Weapon Upgrades

Only one of each type of upgrade is allowed. Heavy weapons cannot be upgraded

Low light levels reduce ranged accuracy by 50%; vision clouding effects like fog or smoke reduce visibility range to 20m, beyond which ranged accuracy = 0

Pistols and assault rifles are noticable when fired from 50 m away, snipers and heavy weapons are noticeable from 100m away
