Every character has the following common abilities:

  • Stringth: Affects melee damage and carry weight
  • Accuracy: Affects the ability to hit enemies
  • Agility: Affects evasion, climbing and other acrobatics
  • Intelligence: Affects knowledge, perception and spellcasting
  • Charisma: Affects NPC interactions
  • Corruption Resistance: Affects warp and pther mental resistances
  • Constitiotion: Affects physical resisrances
