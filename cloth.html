Runes

Runes function as weapon enchantments and are backwards compatible - meaning if the armour can hold one rare rune, you can put in a common or uncommon one instead. Be warned, runes are permanent and can only be improved (common -> uncommon, etc), not interchanged with other runes. Same type runes may be inscribed into an armour
