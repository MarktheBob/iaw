Tau initially were confined to one world and on the verge of extinction as wars raged on T'au. Then the strange Etherial race came to the warring sides and convinced them to join forces for the Greater Good. What followed was the rapid expansion of the Tau Empire and a boom in technology as Tau divided into five castes - Fire warriors, Earth builders, Water diplomats and merchants, Air pilots and Etherial Leaders. However due to their inability to sense the Warp (thus no psykers) their spaceships could not navigate the Warp and hence the expansions were slower and spanned less distance than Imperium Expansions.

The Etherials knew that at some point Tau would face a greater and more powerful foe than Tau could defeat on their own, so when the news came of an alliance between Men and Eldar, Tau were eager to participate, sending their warriors to train with both races, lending their long range firepower to Alliance squads.
