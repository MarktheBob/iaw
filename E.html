Eldar are an ancient race that still remembers the necrontyr race before their sleep. They have only two purposes in this Universe: destroy all traces of Necrons that warred with their creators, the Old Ones; and to kill Slaanesh, a god(dess) born from the sins of Eldar, creating the Eye of Terror.

All Eldar are connected to the Warp, so a vast majority of them have psyker abilities like seeing the future and doing basic spells. They were originally a peaceful race, but the constant advancements of younger races made them take up arms. All eldar warriors follow the Path of the Warrior and specialise in some sort of combat, be it long range or melee.

Aspects and their perks (to be chosen at Rank 2) - only for aspect warrior path

  • Dire Avengers
    • Access to Assault Rifles, Pistols, Swords and Daggers
  • Howling Banshees
    • Access to Pistols (only after Rank 4), Swords, Spears and Daggers
    • Bonuses: +1 strength every rank, +2 accuracy every rank while melee dual wielding, +5 m movement distance
    • Penalties: -4 ranged accuracy, cannot use spells
  • Dark Reapers
    • Access to Pistols, Heavy Weapons (Rank 3 = Basic, Rank 4 = Advanced, Rank 5 = Legendary) and Daggers, can have medium Assault Rifle at Rank 2
    • Bonuses: +1 accuracy every rank, ignores 1*rank armour
    • Penalties: -2 strength in melee combat
  • Warp Spiders
    • Access to Pistols, Assault Rifles and Daggers
    • Bonuses: +1*rank damage against light-armoured troops, after Rank 4 can dual wield assaut rifles (semi-auto mode only), access to teleporters after Rank 3
    • Penalties: -5m movement distance, -2 strength in melee combat
  • Rangers
    • Access to Pistols, Sniper Rifles and Daggers
    • Bonuses: +1*rank accuracy every rank, + 2*rank stealth every rank
    • Penalties: -2 strength in melee combat, cannot use spells
