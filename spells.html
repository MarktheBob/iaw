Character Creation Rules and Guidelines

Spells and Enchantments


Spells have a minimum Intelligence stat, so if your stat is below that value, you cannot learn the spell

A Nat 1 on a spell will deal corruption damage equivalent to the damage output of the spell, or if the spell is non-damaging, deals Intelligence threshold/3 corruption. A Nat 20 spell can either deal 50% more damage or remove caster corruption equal to 50% of dealt damage (Intelligence threshold/3). Non-psykers cannot learn any spells, except for Eldar, who have the racial ability to cast spells. A psyker may cast Intelligence number of spells per day, with the Eldar having a bonus number of spells. A spell can be prepared as a rune instead that can be instacast at any time, but it costs 2 spell slots instead.

Psykers can only learn 1 Elemental and 1 Mental discipline

All spells ignore armour, max range of spells is 200m unless stated otherwise
