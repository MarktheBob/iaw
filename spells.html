Spell Tomes allow psykers to learn new spells of the same level or below their indicated level. Once the spell is learned the Tome becomes blank and cannot be used again or sold

Scrolls contain spells that can only be used once per scroll. All Rank 3 and above characters can use Level 1 scrolls. 1 standard action to cast scroll, doubletap perk does not apply. Scrolls cost 1/3 of the indicated cost, up to level and including lvl 3 spells

Each spell requires a certain time before casting ("Charge" section), so the psyker can absorb the required energy for the spell. You can change your mind after the required ammount of energy is absorbed and either cast a different spell or carry on absorbing to cast higher level spells

When entering combat, a psyker has enough energy to cast a spell that requires Charge 1

A movement action counts as 0.5 of a standard action when absorbing energy to cast a spell

Enchantments can only be placed on items that can be enchanted (Wraithbone items normally). One enchantment per item, cannot disenchant
