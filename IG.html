Imperial Guardsmen are the core of the Imperium's army - they are very easy to train and number billions across the Empire, hence only the best (or luckiest) survive the first month of warfare to raise through the ranks as fast as they can, as their life span averages at 80 years. They are easily adaptable to changes, can survive with minimal equipment and bad weapons, and believe that the Emperor is the only salvation for Mankind.

When the call for arms sonded, the High Lords of Terra sent many recruits to train with xenos races, thus making Imperial Guardsmen the most common solder within mixed regiments. As their gear and weapons were cheap and of low quality, at first the morality rate was very high. Not that it mattered, as Imperium had billions of people to summon for the army. However, as Mundum Congregatium developed, more and more common guardsmen survived their first campains. Moreover, their companions started paying attention to frail guardsmen, often saving them from enemy fire.
