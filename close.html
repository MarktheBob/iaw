Character Creation Rules and Guidelines

Close Combat Weapons

Critical = Natural die roll to get critical damage (2x damage)

One Handed = Daggers (D) + Swords (W), Two handed = Spears (R) + Hammers (M) + Staves (S)

It should be noted that Eldar weapons deal lower damage than Humnan weapons but have a better crit chance. Tau have no melee combat weapons to contribute

Please note that if you roll a Crit but you do not manage to hit the target when comparing melee accuracy vs enemy dodge, you merely hit the target, not land a critical hit

  • Common = C
  • Uncommon = U
  • Rare = R
  • Legendary = L
  • Unique = Q
