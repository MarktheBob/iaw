Character Creation Rules and Guidelines

Ranged Weapons

Critical = Natural die roll to get critical damage (2x damage)

One Handed = Daggers (T) + Pistols (P), Two handed = Assault Rifles (A) + Sniper Rifles (N) + Heavy Weapons (H)

It should be noted that Eldar weapons deal lower damage than Humnan weapons but have a better crit chance. Tau weapons same damage as Eldar weapons but greater ranges

Range = effective shooting range. Beyond that you lose 1 ranged accuracy per 5m until you hit tripple the effective range, beyond which the weapon does not fire.

There are many types of ammo in the game, more is explained in the Accessories tab

Note that a Crit roll beyond the effective range guarantees a hit, but if the target manages to dodge, it deals regular damage instead of double damage

Flamer, Heavy Flamer, Incinerator and Multimelta are Flame based weapons. Plasma Pistol, Plasma Gun, Plasma Cannon, Barrage Plasma Pistol and Barrage Plasma Gun are Plasma based weapons

  • Uncommon = U
  • Rare = R
  • Legendary = L
  • Unique = Q
