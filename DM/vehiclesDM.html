Passengers count as people wearing "Medium" or worse armour. Those with Good, Epic and Legendary Armour occupy space equivalent to 2 normal passengers. XV8 cannot use small land vehicles as transportation, and in other vehicles occupies 4 seats

Hover vehicles fly at the height of 2 m as they have anti-gravity generators. They cannot be hit by most landmines

Land vehicles can be hit by all landmines, but they can hit enemies and friends, dealing 1d6*distance covered this turn/5 damage [think of it as 1d6*speed]

V Fast covers 80 m per standard action and 20 m per movement action; Fast covers 65 m per standard action and 15 m per movement action; Medium covers 50 m per standard action and 10 m per movement action; Slow covers 40 m per standard action and 5 m per movement action

P = Pilot; G = Gunner, is optional

For vehicle weapons and ammo, see this page, with ST = Small Turret, MT = Medium Turret, HT = Heavy Turret and VHT = Super Heavy Turret; some vehicles have a fixed gun that cannot be replaced; unless otherwise indicated, all fixed weapons cannot be rotated and are facing forwrds to be fired by the pilot, all side weapons can be rotated

Armour classes: V Light gives + 6 Armour bonus, Light gives + 12 Armour Bonus, Medium gives + 20 Armour Bonus, Heavy gives + 30 Armour Bonus, Super Heavy gives + 50 Armour Bonus; each small vehicle has 10 health points, each large vehicle has 30 health points
