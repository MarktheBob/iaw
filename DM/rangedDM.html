"*" means charges need to be purchased instead of ammo clips, normally used for plasma weapons

"^" means special ammo needs to be purchased, e.g. rockets

"O" means single shot - reload (movement action) necessary after each shot, reloading clip (standard action) is considered as reloading bullet as well

"S" means semi-automatic - reload (standard action) necesary after clip runs out

"A" means automatic - reload (standard action) necessary after clip runs out; can fire 1 bullet at no penalty or 3 bullets at - 6 accuracy and - 25% damage, dealing damage to up to 3 different enemies (have to hit enemy with highest armour rating to hit the rest, no need to roll damage for each enemy)

A clip reload is a standard action

Unless otherwise indicated, only Legendary Weapons can damage tanks at 75% damage, indicated weapons deal 100% damage (indicated with "can deal damage to tanks")
