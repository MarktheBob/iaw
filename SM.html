Adeptus Astartes, or Space Marines, were founded by the Emperor himself during the formation of the Empire. They are genetically modified warriors standing taller than humans, designed for one purpose: war. They can survive in any environment and atmosphere, run faster and fo r much longer. They have special implanted organs and the Gene-Seed of their Chapter's Founder (Primarch), which differentiates them from ordinary humans. Moreover, the harsh recruiting process ensures that only the best humans survive to be transformed into Space Marines.

Space Marines guarded Imperium since the 30th Millenium, each loyalist chapter fighting and winning multitudes of battles against both xenos races and their traitor brothers, Chaos Marines. When the call to arms sounded, most Chapters were opposed to the idea of fighting alongside their past enemies, but eventually Space Wolves, Salamanders, White Scars, Deathwatch and eventually Ultramarines started to send some of their recruits to train with Alliance squads.
