Adeptus Sororitas, or more commonly Sisters of Battle, are the female equivalent of Space Marines. Clad in heavy Armour, armed with bolters, chain swords and their belief in the Emperor of Mankind the pose a serious threat to most enemies on the battlefield. They are trained fighters and normally carry out the role of tanks due to their heavy Armour and powerful close combat weapons, however it is not uncommon for them to carry heavy bolters and rocket launchers, or rarely be equipped with jet packs.

Generally they carry out the role of protecting the nobles of Imperium, like the High Lords of Terra, as Space Marines are too valuable and impatient to do that kind of duty. Upon hearing of the alliance, the Abbess decided to send some recruits to have several sisters trained in working with other races, effectivel learning their weaknesses and strengths in order to counter any potential threats to their proteges. Also, since Adeptus Sororitas is part of the Ecclesiarchy, and hence collaborate with the Inquisition, they welcome any and all knowledge regarding xenos races.
